First, allow microphone access. This will make a random sequence of 8 notes appear on the grand staff. You should then start playing the notes on your instrument

Website will automatically use your microphone to determine whether you are playing the correct note: if you are, the note will light up green.

Once a note lights up you may continue and play the next note.

Once every note is played a new random sequence of 8 notes is generated. Rinse and repeat. Read and play the notes as fast as you can!
